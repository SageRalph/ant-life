Tile Guide
Gold Trail Encourages Workers to go to the area
Brown Soil Basic building material
Yellow Sand Falls into heaps
Grey Stone Can't be moved
Blue Water Dangerous to Workers, Queens, Eggs, and Pests
Green Plant Grows over time, used to grow more Fungus
Teal Fungus Food for queens, grows by consuming Plants
Red Worker Move things around and fight Pests
Purple Queen Eats fungus to produce Eggs
White Egg Will eventually hatch into a Worker or Queen
Black Debris Various dead things, good fertiliser for Plants
Pink Pest Hunts for Eggs, but Workers can fight them
Hints
  • Don't let all your queens die!
  • Eggs have a small chance to hatch into an extra queen
  • Hatch more ants by supplying your queens with fungus
  • Grow more fungus by supplying it with plant material
  • Queens leave the last few bits of fungus so it can be regrown
  • Workers spread plants and fungus around when moving near them
  • Use trails to make workers go where you need them
  • Plants grow slower when crowded, small plants grow faster
  • Fungus will only grow underground
  • Water evaporates from moving, the sun, and plants
  • Water only spreads sideways if it can't move downwards
  • Rain comes at regular intervals, but varies in quantity
  • A roof, drainage pits, and U-bends help to survive the rain
  • If you take losses, debris make great fertilizer for plants
